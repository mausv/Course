Like us

A little bit of history

We are a very small group of people who just want to make games... and money. Started back in 2007 with two brothers, knowing sh*t about making games, but as time passed they learned and learned until they knew just enough. Then, in late 2010 a friend jumped in and they said, "Hey, why don't we just start all over and make a serious, market-targeted game?" And here we are now.
So... yeah, we are indie.
We are Splash Games Studio.
